UMD Annual Sales Summit

The Sales Summit is an opportunity for both personal and professional growth. This workshop includes speakers who give presentations throughout the day, and a lunch panel where participants are provided with a free lunch and networking opportunities. Students are given opportunities to initiate conversation with professionals and prospecting employers. No matter who you are or what your aspirations may be, you can be certain to walk away with the knowledge and skills necessary in the sales industry.

What will it provide?

The event at University of Minnesota-Duluth encourages students to think differently, refine their skills, and elevate how they present themselves in today’s market. Through practical insights, real-world strategies, and meaningful connections, attendees gain the tools and confidence needed to stand out, create value, and lead with purpose.

Whether pursuing a new career path, strengthening existing skills, or exploring opportunities for development, the Sales Summit serves as a catalyst for growth and transformation. It offers a chance to take the next step and discover what it means to truly set oneself apart.
