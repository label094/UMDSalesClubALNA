Who are we?

The Sales Club is designed to give students of any major a leg up on gaining real-world knowledge and career opportunities in sales. Students participating in Sales Club can expect to have opportunities at their disposal such as sales competitions, networking events, leadership opportunities, interview preparation, and community involvement. Our mission statement is to connect students to sales careers. Given that 75% of business school graduates find themselves in customer-facing roles post-graduation, the Sales Club is providing key resources to keep Bulldogs ahead of the competition. We are not just a club, we are the bridge from college to career for students, run by students.
